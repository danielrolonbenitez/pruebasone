Assembly Registration

  • Is required for participation in the Assembly a contribution of U$S 1.100. It includes flight from Buenos Aires to Bariloche and Bariloche to Buenos Aires; transfer airport – hotel to hotel – airport; four night’s accommodation at the Llao Llao Hotel (www.llaollao.com); full board (breakfast, meals, infusions, cocktails, dinners), a tourist visit; night out.

    For those flights where the passenger must wait at the airport more than five hours for Buenos Aires – Bariloche connection, may be requested a free of charge room in Buenos Aires.

    This will remain until 15th February 2016. After this date the value will be U$S 1.500 until 21st February 2016. From that date may register but flight availability Buenos Aires - Bariloche and Bariloche – Buenos Aires cannot be secured.
